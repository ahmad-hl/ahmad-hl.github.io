About Me

I am postdoctoral fellow at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Division of Emerging Interdisciplinary Areas (EMIA), working with Prof. PAN HUI. My research interests include Adaptive Network-aware streaming, Scalable Visual Computing, Cloud-rendered Mobile/XR Gaming.

I received my PhD degree in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, department of Computer Science Engineering. My Ph.D. thesis is titled "Reliable and Real-time Content Streaming for Edge and Cloud Computing"
